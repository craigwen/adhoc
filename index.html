about

Ad Hoc is a growing ensemble of vocalists and musicians living and performing in New York City. We explore the traditions of early music and also play with its possibilities, drawing from an eclectic array of influences to keep it fresh. Our core is a group of dedicated residents, and we’re always looking to bring in skilled, creative guests to work with us on upcoming projects.

Interested in joining Ad Hoc as a resident or guest? Drop us a line.
